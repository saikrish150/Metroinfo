    Metro Travel Guidelines

    Do's:


    • ✔ Plan your journey ahead of time and check the train schedule to avoid any delays.
    • ✔ Keep your belongings close to you at all times and avoid displaying expensive items like jewelry or electronic gadgets.
    • ✔ Stand behind the yellow line on the platform until the train arrives and wait for passengers to exit before boarding.
    • ✔ Hold onto handrails or grab handles when standing inside the train to maintain balance.
    • ✔ Be considerate of other passengers and offer your seat to those in need, such as elderly or pregnant passengers.
    • ✔ Follow any announcements or instructions given by the train operator or station staff.
    • ✔ Keep the metro station and train clean and tidy by disposing of trash in designated bins.

    Don'ts:


    • ❌ Do not run or push other passengers while boarding or exiting the train.
    • ❌ Do not block the train doors and allow passengers to exit before boarding 🚷.
    • ❌ Do not eat or drink on the train or in the station, except in designated areas🚭.
    • ❌ Do not play loud music or use your mobile phone in speaker mode inside the train 🔕.
    • ❌ Do not use foul language or engage in disruptive behavior that may disturb other passengers.
    • ❌ Do not use emergency exits or break any safety rules or regulations.

    Precautions:


    • ❗ 😷 Always carry a face mask and wear it properly throughout the journey to prevent the spread of germs and viruses.
    • ❗ Use hand sanitizers or wash your hands frequently, especially after touching any surfaces.
    • ❗Avoid touching your face, mouth, or eyes while traveling and cover your mouth and nose when coughing or sneezing.
    • ❗ Maintain physical distancing whenever possible and avoid crowded trains or stations.
    • ❗ Use contactless payment methods or smart cards to avoid physical contact with ticket machines or counters.
    • ❗ If you feel unwell or exhibit any symptoms, avoid traveling and seek medical attention immediately.

    🤗Happy journey🚆
